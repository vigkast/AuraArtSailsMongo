Announcing Third Edition of The Art Enclave at UBM Index Fairs 2016
 
Enthused by strong response to the earlier two editions of The Art Enclave, Aura Art Development Pvt Ltd (AADPL) is pleased to announce the Third Edition of The Art Enclave, to be held at Index Fairs 2016, from October 13-16, 2016, at Bombay Convention & Exhibition Centre, Hall 1, Off Western Express Highway, Goregaon (East), Mumbai 400 063.




AADPL invites Galleries and Artists from across the world to exhibit Paintings, Sculptures, Prints and Photographs at The Art Enclave in Index Fairs 2016, as well as leading providers of Art Infrastructure Products and Services. Links have been provided to the Application Docket, the Exhibitor Application Form and Hall Layout for The Art Enclave at Index Fairs 2016. Look forward to hearing promptly from you to book the best location in The Art Enclave and to avail of enhanced benefit from the multi-pronged promotion strategy.

To recap, AADPL has entered into long-term collaboration with UBM Index Trade Fairs Pvt Ltd in 2014, for incorporating an exclusive Fine Art Section in Index Fairs, details of which are provided at our Application Docket - Invitation for Participation. Index Fairs is India's monumental event for International interiors, architecture and design for the last 27 years. It is the only Indian trade fair to be on the international tradefair map and has introduced many "first time in India" products, making it imperative for professional specifiers from across the country to visit the fair.

We would be glad to involve you in this interesting initiative, which is in line with Aura Art vision to fill the gaps in the Indian Art Industry - by working closely with the design space to meet the Art requirements of various architects, interior designers, hotels, hospitals, corporates, real estate developers, home, office and trade buyers and professional specifiers from across the country.
