Reconstruction of experiences



Reconstruction of experiences
- Hindustan Times

“... Experience enlivened and observed that is my art...” says Bhushen. Indeed so. He reconstructs inexplicably exuberant feeling and looks at them with pensive wonderment... the moment is reborn, overwhelmed by uncomfort of an ecstasy—pang of a remorse—enchantment of a mystery or simply the burden of existence. The moment outlives the technical endeavour which in itself is free from mannerist sophistry, subordinated sincerely to the purpose. He is communicative, social, his works are substantive, thematic in the literary sense, but authentically true to the medium. He is non conformist, without being arbitrary. He is thoughtful and disciplined. There is dream like quality about his work, not as a result of obscurity of forms but emanating from juxtaposition of identifiable objects which Bhushen harmonizes with overall structure. For him dream is a state, not symbol. His rendering is sappy, sensitive just short of sentimentality.
