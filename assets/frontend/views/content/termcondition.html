  1. Submission of Artworks on the Website:
    1. As an artist, you may submit listings for only those Original Works of Art that you have created and desire to sell through the Aura Art Website (“Website”).
    2. In order that data submitted by you be accepted for listing on the Website, you must provide Aura Art with all the information requested for, on the applicable page of the Website.
    3. Please be accurate while submitting data for listing of Artworks. The Buyer reserves the right to return an Artwork purchased by him / her / it if there is a material difference between the colour or physical condition of the actual Artwork as compared to its image, as submitted by you.     5. Please remember that the appearance of listings in Search Results may be based on different factors including keywords. Hence, although entering keywords while submitting data is not mandatory it may be useful for you to enter multiple keywords directly relevant to the Art Work being submitted by you.

  2. Listing of Art Works on the Website:

    Image of Art Work(s) once approved by the Curatorial Team will remain on the Website for a minimum period of 180 days from the date of approval, for display and sale, on consignment basis.

  3. Sales Commission, Taxes and Payment Terms:
    1. In the event of Sale, Aura Art will retain a pre agreed margin from the Selling Price and pay the balance amount due to you within 21 days after the Art Work is delivered to the Buyer or full payment received from the Buyer, whichever is later. The amount due to you will be paid to you either by transfer / RTGS / NEFT to your Bank Account, details of which are provided by you on your Profile Page, or you will be sent a Demand Draft / Account Payee Cheque via Courier / Speed Post / Post.
    2. In the event you are charging VAT or any other indirect taxes to Aura Art on sale, you will have to provide a Tax Invoice and other incidental declarations Aura Art may require from you.
  4. Delivery of Artwork(s):
    1. Upon intimation of confirmation of sale of your Artwork by Aura Art, you shall promptly commence packing the Artwork(s) in the form indicated by Aura Art. Painting(s) shall be safely packed in the form advised by Aura Art ie Rolled works packed in a PVC Pipe or Stretched / Framed works packed and created in a wooden box . Sculpture shall be appropriately packed in a Wooden Crate. The cost of packing and shipping the Artwork to the Aura Art Office at Mumbai will be borne by you.
    2. In the event a Buyer returns an Artwork purchased by him / her / it due to reasons stated in Point No. 1.3 above, you will be responsible and liable for any loss / expenses incurred by Aura Art by virtue of such returns, including but not limited to costs incurred in packing and shipping artworks to the buyer and the cost of repacking and shipping the Artwork from the Buyer’s destination to your address.
  5. Failure to Deliver Artwork(s):

    If for any reason whatsoever an Artwork submitted by you and listed on the Website is sold directly by you, you will promptly inform Aura Art about the same, but not later than 48 hours of the sale. In such cases, Aura Art will be entitled to half the pre agreed margin on the listed price. In the event you do not inform Aura Art about its sale or for any other reason whatsoever, an Art Work is enquired for by Aura Art or is sold by Aura Art, and you are unable to promptly deliver the Art Work , Aura Art is in such case entitled to the full pre agreed margin on the listed price.

  6. Authentication Certificate:

    Upon intimation of confirmation of sale of your Artwork by Aura Art, you will have to send the Original Authentication Certificate signed by you along with the Artwork.
