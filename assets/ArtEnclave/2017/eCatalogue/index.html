Radha PatelBooth No AE 7
   
Born in Gujarat in 1976, Radha Patel attained her Diploma in Painting from IS College Fine Arts, Vallabh Vidyanagar, in 1997. She has participated in several group exhibitions since then. Her creations include combinations of various mediums like acrylic/oil colours on canvas, ink on canvas, ceramic on wood/clay, clay moulding, metal on wood, and more. She conceives an ability of producing conversational art, which makes her creations a discourse with her inner self. Such an instinct as an artist gives her art depth and sensibility of emotions. Her creativity, thus, is a product of her retrospection of life. In broader sense, she can be justified as an expressionist, who ‘sought to express the meaning of emotional experience rather than the physical reality’. Overall impact of her works is fabulous and thoughtful. She has guts to feel the cosmic self of everything she sees or experiences, and that makes her expressions more elevated in artistic manner.

Taco Visions Pvt LtdBooth No AE 16
   
Taco Visions Pvt Ltd is a creative enterprise, who have been successfully creating 3Dimensional Lenticular Art for renowned artists, architects, builders and interior decorators.

Lenticular printing is a technology in which the image is separated into layers, with defined visual depth, sliced into strips, printed and bonded with a lenticular sheet, to create an illusion of depth, and an amazing visual experience.

Taco Visions has been awarded as the best 3D printer in the world, in the category of 'Artistic Works' at DRUPA, 2012, Dusseldorf, Germany. In the year 2016, once again they were nominated for this award in the same category, which was again a great achievement.

They are also the proud recipients of all three Gold, Silver and Bronze awards in lenticular printing conducted by IAPHC 2010. (International Association of Printing House Craftsman) held in Minneapolis, USA. Their name also features in the 2012 edition of “The Limca book of records” for conceptualization and installing the longest (68 ft. x 8 ft.) animated 3D (Lenticular) wall mural at the prestigious Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital & Medical Research Institute, Mumbai.

This 3-Dimensional Lenticular art enlivens the artist's paintings, with its depth, flip and animation effect, which would be entirely impossible on any other media. These 3-Dimensional Lenticular Art is suitable for all interiors, from homes, offices, schools, colleges to hospitals and corporate murals. Even the builder's catalogue printed with this 3-Dimensional Lenticular Art, adds an extra dimension by catching the reader's attention and making it more proactive.
