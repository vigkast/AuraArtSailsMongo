PO10TIAL is an organization established to encourage artists and provide support through a strong collaborative effort. The intention is to provide a platform to people to explore their creativity and reintegrate art within the social fabric, in a meaningful way. Suvra is a freelance artist and founding member of dream project, Art@SFactor, for creating and promoting artists and Art@SFactor's HeART project - fund raising for charities while promoting emerging artists. Suvra very eloquently puts forth his jouney - "The search for the artist in me began since I was born. I have wanted to paint ever since I can remember. Painting is my true self, soul, breath and blood. My meditative concentration, solitude and a state of detachment (vairagya) leads me to art. But the most important factor that drives me is my strong belief in myself. I draw inspiration from my own perception of life, nature, world, nature and my soul's response to it all. My vision is beyond the obvious and manifests itself on canvas through colour, form and balance. My art is my meditation and I often find myself becoming at one with it and I feel as though a greater power is guiding me to paint. I often work with the female form as it embodies softness, love, compassion and aesthetics. This is my way of spreading tranquillity and peace in a fast paced mad world."
