    • Born in 1983 in Andhra Pradesh, Veguri Ravindra Babu completed Bachelor of Fine Arts from Andhra University at Vishakhapatnam in 2006. He proceeded to complete Master of Fine Arts from Jamia Milia Islamia University, New Delhi in 2008. He qualified for Lecturership in Visual Arts by clearing UGC NET in 2007. He was awarded HRD National Scholarship in 2006. Ravindra Babu has presented papers at seminars and for publications. He is academically inclined as well and has registered with Andhra University, Vishakhapatnam, in 2014, to pursue Ph.D in Visual Arts. Ravindra Babu has participated in several significant group exhibitons at prestigious venues and has held solo shows, in different cities of India. He also attended the International Art Residency Program HOM ART TRANS at Malaysia in November 2014.
