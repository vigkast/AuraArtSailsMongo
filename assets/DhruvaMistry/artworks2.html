Reclining Woman   1986-1988

Proposals for a large monumental work for the British Rail were invited in 1986. Eventually the project was not realized in time. In 1988, the Glasgow Garden Festival invited number of artists for installations on site. The sand and cement work was made at the Govan Shipyard in Glasgow.

At the end of the Festival the work was acquired by a private collector and changed hands in 2002. Now it is in a private collection in Sutton, England.
