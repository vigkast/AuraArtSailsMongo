Established in 1971, Cymroza Art Gallery is more than a prime exhibition space. Located in South Mumbai, it has been closely associated with the development of contemporary Indian art.

It regularly holds interesting lecture programmes, conducts workshops and discussions on various forms of art, laying stress on education for both the academically inclined and laypersons. Cymroza has provided a platform for aspiring artists, who have always availed of the opportunities provided to interact with the art fraternity at various levels.
While continuing to be a centre of activity for artists, connoisseurs of art, art-lovers, and the public, the promoters of the gallery see beyond art; their vision encompassing the study of the continuing development of Art as a subject influenced by culture, tradition and circumstances.

Gallery Time and SpaceBooth No AE 12
   
Gallery Time and Space, established in 1997, has grown into a premier gallery of Bangalore, with a reputation for integrity and stability which applies to the vision of the gallery as well as its relationship with the many collectors who have patronized it over the years. The gallery's collection comprises of works of established masters as well as young and talented artists. These artists have been chosen and fostered by the gallery. The classical world of art is the gallery's forte and within this broad frame work, the gallery showcases art that is authentic and rooted in the ground beyond short-term trends and such that will withstand the vicissitudes of time.
