Feedback Comments
Mrs Nawaz Modi Singhania, Fitness-n-lifestyle expert and artist
"It's a laudable effort that Index and Aura Art have initiated to create The Art Enclave which will provide an integrated platform to reach fine art out to interior designers and architects from across the country. This platform will enable them to engage and collaborate with a spectrum of artists and bring good quality art to their projects, whether homes, offices, hotels or even hospitals." Click to view the entire speech
Ar Sanjay Puri and Ar Nina Puri, Sanjay Puri Architects
"Index... I have been coming to ever since its inception, and I think it's a very good exhibition. It puts everything in perspective in a very nice way. You get to see all the new products and they are all sequentially done. And this time, of the maximum interest was the art gallery spaces they have created... very very nicely done."
"I think Index is very good because it's compact. It's got everything. Nothing unnecessary is there. And yes (as he says), the art part is a very good part."
Feryna Wazheir, Actress
"You know I am surprised it's such a beautiful event and they've had so many great artists here... the film that we are promoting here, Rang Rasiya, is based on one of the fathers, founders of art in india. So it's great to be part of this. Its all about art and design. It's a great ambience to be a part of. I really enjoyed it - a mixture of design, art and architecture. I think this is the first year they've had various artists. It's really great. It's been a fun exhilarating experience and a way to discover a lot of really good artists."
Ar Kalhan Mattoo and Ar Santha Mattoo, Planet 3 Studios
"Index... we've been coming for a long time. This year also it's been very nice. It's grown, its more exciting, lot of energy here, lot of people we get to see, new things, new products. This time the art section is very nice, very interesting. It's quite a nice show to visit. And Sylvia and Liyakat put up a good show every time..."
Ar Sunil Gambani, Architect Hafeez Contractor
"I have been coming for ever. I have been a regular visitor at Index. It's been good. As smooth as it always used to be. I quite like the art section also that you guys have put up this time. That's a good way of connecting with a lot of agencies under one roof."
Ar Leena Kumar, Kumar Consultants
"In the exhibition of course, you know the art section that you've added on is very interesting and I hope we can use a lot of the art that I've seen. The exhibition itself is good because a lot of exhibitions that keep coming, especially in Bangalore, every month there's an exhibition; but in Index, I do look forward to, because the exhibitors are good, the products are good, and there is always something I take back and learn from..."
Shami Goregaokar, GA Design
"My experience has been good in the sense that I got to see some new products. I have come across new items, I have come across new artists because there is a big artist lounge as well. Yes .. It's been good."
