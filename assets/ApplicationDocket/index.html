About Us

Aura Art development Pvt Ltd ("AADPL") aspires to be a leading art platform, undertaking various initiatives with a view to benefit the entire Indian art industry. Towards this goal, AADPL undertakes various activities to increase awareness for art and extend the boundaries of where art is displayed. Besides, AADPL also assists collectors in managing their collections by providing integrated Art Infrastructure Solutions.

UBM Index TradeFairs Pvt Ltd ("UBMITPL") has been organizing UBM Index TradeFairs, India's monumental event for International interiors, architecture and design for the last 27 years. It is the only Indian trade fair to be on the international tradefair map and has introduced many "first time in India" products, making it imperative for professional specifiers from across the country to visit the fair. UBMITPL is now part of Universal Business Media (UBM) Group, a UK-based world leader in tradeshows.

The Art Enclave was formed out of a long term collaboration between AADPL and UBMITPL, for incorporating an exclusive Fine Art Section in UBM Index Fairs. The first two editions of The Art Enclave - comprising of leading Galleries and Artists from across the world and displaying quality paintings, sculptures, photographs and prints, alongwith Art Infrastructure Solution Providers - were received with great enthusiasm by space designers, corporates as well as collectors.

Booth Description

Booth
Booths are made from Octonorm Systems employed worldwide in Exhibitions. Each panel of booth wall is 1 metre (m) in width and 8 feet in height. Booths of 2m depth are offered in the first two rows and booths of 3m depth are offered in the next two rows. The size of the smallest booth is 2m x 3m (6 sqm) in the first two rows and 3m x 3m (9 sqm) in the next two rows. Both types of booths are expandable by adding 1 metre wide panels to the centre-back wall.
